Getting Started

Installation

  • Download lucid by clicking here.
  • Login to your domain cpanel and upload the zipped luzid file.
  • Unzip the file to the /www directory or your root directory.
  • Visit your domain on your browser and install lucid by using your gmail or facebook account, or entring your details in the form.
  • Click install and you are set

FAQ

  • Q: What is the difference "microblog" and "blog" mode?
    A: Microblog mode is typically for small content. It doesn't have a title or the What You See Is What You Get (WYSIWYG) Editor. The blog is for larger content, features a title field and the WYSIWYG Editor.
  • Q: How do I switch or toggle microblog/blog modes?
    A: The default mode is microblog. Blog is automatically toggled when the input field exceeds 100 characters. There is no other way to switch modes.
  • Q: How can a guest visit or access my page?
    A: After installing Lucid on your custom domain, simply share your domain URL with others.
